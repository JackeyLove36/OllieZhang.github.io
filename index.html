Research Interests

My long-term research aims to design the computational tools or deep learning models to solve the hardest problem with achieving high performance in computing and provable guarantees in algorithm. With solid background in numerical PDEs, theoretical computer science and optimal control, I plan to explore unsolvable problem in Multi-Path Planning Problem & Optimal Control (EECS), High-Dimensional Hamilton-Jacobian Bellman Equation (Applied Math), and Earth Turbulance Modeling (Plantery).
